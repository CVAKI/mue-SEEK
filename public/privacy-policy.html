Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Welcome to μSEEK ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our music streaming service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile information.
  • Payment Information: If you purchase a premium subscription, we collect payment information through our secure payment processors.
  • Music Preferences: We collect information about the music you listen to, create playlists with, and interact with.
  • Device Information: We collect information about the devices you use to access our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Personalize your music experience
  • Process your transactions
  • Communicate with you about products, services, offers, and events
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our services

3. Sharing Your Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Other users as directed by you (such as when you share playlists)
  • Law enforcement or other third parties when required by law

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and receive a copy of your personal information
  • Rectify inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your personal information
  • Data portability

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@museek.com.
